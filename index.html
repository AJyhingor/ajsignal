EMA/SMA are computed on raw price closes (not RSI). The dot position is the price's normalized location within its recent trading range, scaled onto 12–88 for display (clamped so it never leaves the bar) — a MAJOR signal is a crossover happening at or beyond the 12/88 edge; a MINOR signal is a crossover happening in the middle zone. This tool only reads public market data; it does not place trades. Not financial advice.
